Summary of Changes

Light Mode Strategy:

  • Changed from *-50 to *-100 backgrounds (more saturated)
  • Changed from *-700 to *-900 text (darker, better contrast)
  • Changed from *-200 to *-300 borders (more visible)

Dark Mode Strategy:

  • Changed from *-900/30 to *-500/10 backgrounds (lighter base, subtle opacity)
  • Changed from *-400 to *-300 text (lighter, more vibrant)
  • Changed from *-800 to *-500/30 borders (more visible with opacity)

Result:

Much better visual hierarchy, improved readability, and proper contrast ratios that match the original inline style implementation.
